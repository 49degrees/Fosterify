Key Insights

  • Over 50% of students are not fully aware of available opportunities
  • Mentorship has the lowest access among all opportunity types
  • A gap exists between access and participation
  • The biggest barriers are lack of time and awareness
  • Many students do not believe opportunities are equally accessible

Action Plan

Fosterify will develop a centralized platform where all academic and extracurricular opportunities are clearly listed, improving awareness and accessibility.

About

Fosterify is a student-led initiative using data to identify and reduce inequality in access to opportunities.
